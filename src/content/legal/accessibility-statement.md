## Introduction

At Grant Kennedy Web Design & Consulting, we are committed to ensuring that our website and digital services are accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We believe technology should be inclusive and strive to deliver an online experience that meets a wide range of accessibility needs.

## Our Accessibility Standards

We follow recognized accessibility guidelines and best practices, including:

- Conformance with **WCAG 2.1 AA standards**  
- Accessible design principles in typography, contrast, and navigation  
- Compatibility with assistive technologies such as screen readers  
- Text alternatives for non-text content, including images and icons  
- Regular testing with both automated and manual accessibility tools  

## Continuous Improvements

Accessibility is not a one-time project—it is an ongoing commitment. As technology evolves, we continue to refine and enhance our digital platforms to ensure inclusivity for all users.

## Accessibility Features

- Keyboard-friendly navigation across interactive elements  
- Clear and descriptive headings and links for easy browsing  
- Accessible online forms with properly labeled fields  
- Multilingual support in **English and Japanese** (Coming soon.)
